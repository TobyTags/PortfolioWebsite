IceOut

Founder & CEO (Ongoing)

IceOut combines a physical NFC card with an iOS app to intentionally block distracting apps and websites. Users tap the card to “freeze” their phone, creating a deliberate barrier between impulse and action to reduce screen time and improve focus.

CanSat

CanSat: Team Captain (2023 & 2024)

I helped get going and captained my school’s CanSat team for two years, leading a six-person team to win the regional competition and qualify for the national finals. CanSat is an international engineering competition where teams design, build, and launch a soda-can-sized satellite to collect and transmit environmental data.

In 2023 I was lead CAD designer and in 2024 I was lead programmer. The satellite is designed to collect sensor data during descent and then present that data to judges via a presentation.
